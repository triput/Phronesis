ⓘ What is this?

Avg Score is the mean daily System Stability Index in this window — a capacity/throughput signal against your Settings targets, not gamified points.

Each day scores ~55% completion ratio + ~45% focus ratio (0–100). Bands: Stable (≥70), Behind, Overloaded (focus ≥1.25× target).

Adjust daily completion and focus targets in Settings → General.
